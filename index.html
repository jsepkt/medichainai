How It Works

Our platform simplifies a complex process into four transparent steps, ensuring security and value creation for everyone involved.

1. Contribute Data

Patients securely connect and consent to share their anonymized health data on their own terms.

2. Process & Anonymize

Our Google Cloud pipeline processes and de-identifies the data, making it ready for secure AI training.

3. Train AI Models

Researchers use the data pool to train and validate transparent, life-saving diagnostic AI models.

4. Earn Rewards

Patients and institutions earn $MCAI tokens for their contributions, creating a self-sustaining economy.
